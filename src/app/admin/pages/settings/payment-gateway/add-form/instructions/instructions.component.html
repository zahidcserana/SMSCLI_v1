Setup Instructions

Connecting your Authorize.net account to RentMy is easy! Follow these simple steps to use Authorize.net as the merchant processor for your RentMy transactions.

Step 1: Log into Authorize.net

Step 2: Select “Settings” under the Account

Step 3: Click API Credentials & Keys

Step 4: Copy your API Login ID

Step 5: Create a new transaction key

Step 6: Copy the new transaction key and paste it into the appropriate fields in your RentMy account.

Step 7: Hit the “Submit” button. Your information is saved and now your customers’ payments will be processed through your Authorize.net account.

Setup Instructions

Connecting your PayPal Pro account to RentMy is easy! Follow these simple steps to use Authorize.net as the merchant processor for your Renterval transactions.

Step 1: Log into PayPal and confirm your account type is PayPal Pro.

Step 2: Select ”Profile” next to the logout button.

Step 3: Choose “My selling tools”

Step 4: Click “Update” from the API access link

Step 5: Choose “Manage API credentials” from the NVP/SOAP API integration (Classic)” method

Step 6: Copy the information from the respective fields and paste them to the appropriate fields in your RentMy account.

Step 7: Hit the “Submit” button. Your information is saved and now your customers’ payments will be processed through your PayPal Pro account.

Setup Instructions

Connect Stripe to your RentMy account by following these simple steps:

Step 1: Log in to your Stripe account at Stripe.com.

Step 2: Visit the Developers area.

Step 3: Click “API keys”

Step 4: Copy the Publishable and Secret keys

Step 5: Add keys to your account and click “Submit”
